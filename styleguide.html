    Branding Ideas

    What does the company or offer?

    The company offers a curated selection of high-quality lifestyle products, from fashion and accessories to home decor, providing a one-stop shopping experience for those seeking style and quality.

    Who is the company to potential customers?

    The retail brand is designed to cater to a dynamic and fashion-forward audience seeking affordable yet stylish lifestyle products. We specifically target individuals aged 18 to 35 who embrace the latest trends and value quality in their purchases.

    Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

    The products are carefully chosen for their quality and style, allowing customers to effortlessly elevate their lifestyle. With a commitment to affordability and the latest trends, we make luxury accessible to everyone.

    Assembled Brand Components

    The overall art direction will be timeless, elegant, and minimal.

    Font Family

    Helvetica

    In the world of curated lifestyle products, we choose Helvetica for its universal appeal, ensuring clarity and readability in diverse contexts. This timeless, modern typeface complements our brand's commitment to affordable luxury, targeting a dynamic, fashion-forward audience aged 18 to 35. Versatile and elegant, Helvetica adapts seamlessly across various mediums, reflecting our brand's enduring style and accessibility to all.

    Colour Palettes

    Orange (#f54209) will be used for navigation and hover states.
    Black (#000000) will be used for all text.
    An off white (#f1f2ee) will be used for the background colour of the webpage.

    Potential Imagery/Art Direction

    Our imagery, featuring clean white and black product shots, embodies a clean, minimal aesthetic. This intentional blend creates a visual experience that resonates with simplicity, elegance, and a timeless allure. Welcome to a world where every detail, from color to imagery, harmonizes to evoke a sense of refined simplicity.
